The project is in portuguese and has a manual in Portuguese.

The project consists of the naval division of territory for fishing purpose.

The project grabs a sea witch is divided into territories, witch is then divided into small fishing areas witch can be acquired for explorations by companies through auctions.

The companies bid on the areas every BID is stored in the DB and every auction, company, area, territory and sea is also in the DB.

Insert2 populates the DB.



The project is in MySQL and PHP.
